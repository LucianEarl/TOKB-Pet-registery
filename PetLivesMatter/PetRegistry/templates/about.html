Introduction

We here at Pet Lives Matter believe that pet lives matter. It may seem like unneccesary repetition, but it is true. Your furry family matters, at least as much as actual people. Some would say even more so, but we are are not legally allowed to say that anymore. Pets are important, as they don't hurt people and in fact cannot speak. Sometimes it says more to say nothing than it does to say something. That's why we love pets, they have to listen to you. And they will eat whatever you give them, more so than any human child would.

However, one common issue with pets is that they cannot use the internet or social media. So if they go missing and lose their way, it can be quite difficult for them to come home. You used to have to rely on the kindness of strangers, which is kind of strange. Not only that, but you would likely have to print out many flyers and posters, and go through the dilemma of putting them up around town. If only there was a virtual alternative. Well, thanks to out generous clients at the Auckland council, there is!
