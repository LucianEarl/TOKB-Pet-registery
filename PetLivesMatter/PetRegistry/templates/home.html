Introduction

Welcome to Pets Lives Matter!
Has your pet gone missing? Are you afraid of your beloved pet running away and not being able to find it?
Pets Lives Matter allows you and fellow pet enthusiasts in your neighbour hood register your pets so if they decide to go missing you can alert your local community, making it easier for someone to identify it. If someone finds a lost animal they can easily identify it through our missing pet registry.

How do you use Pets Lives Matter?
Step 1: Register yourself and go through the verification steps
Step 2: Register your pets with the applicable details
Step 3: If your pet has gone missing please go to its profile and label it as missing
What happens now?
An alert will be sent out to other users in your community to look out for it. We are also linked to the SPCA so they will get a notice as well. If your pet is found by the SPCA an alert will be sent to you to contact them.
I have found a lost pet? How do I identify it?
Please go to the missing pet page and fill in the filters, pets matching the description will be shown.
There will be a contact family button, please press this and enter your details. An alert will be sent to the family to contact you.
